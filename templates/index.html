What is EcoDose?

Why EcoDose?

EcoDose addresses the need for sustainable agriculture by recommending precise dosages of Rhizobium-based biofertilizer beads. Rhizobium biofertilizers naturally enhance soil fertility and crop yield by fixing atmospheric nitrogen, reducing the dependence on chemical fertilizers and promoting eco-friendly farming practices.

How It Works

Our AI-powered assistant analyzes real-time soil data, including pH, moisture, and NPK (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium) levels, to calculate the optimal biofertilizer dosage for your crop. By leveraging advanced machine learning models, EcoDose tailors recommendations to your unique soil profile, ensuring efficient and effective nutrient management.

Our Vision

We aim to make precision agriculture accessible to everyone. Our future goals include:

  • Mobile app support for on-the-go recommendations
  • Natural Language Processing (NLP) for rural languages
  • Automated sensor integration for real-time dosing
EcoDose is committed to empowering farmers and agri-enthusiasts with smart, sustainable solutions for a greener tomorrow.
